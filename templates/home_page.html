What is Pollar.me?

Pollar.me is a community-curated survey repository that provides its members with a user-friendly way to host, create, and distribute surveys. With Pollar.me, confidentiality and security is at the forefront - by using CockroachDB as the database of choice, all user data is ensured to be private and encrypted by the database and is only accessible by parties who have been given consent by the user.
